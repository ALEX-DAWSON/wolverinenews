About Wolverine Fitness

Wolverine Fitness is the unofficial website of the Wellington Heights Wolverines. All information is updated by volunteers as time is available, so please be patient if scores are not updated immediately.

Chrome and Safari are 100% supported. Use either of these browsers for the best experience. Firefox is almost perfect, but I have no idea what this site looks like in Internet Explorer.

Wolverine Fitness is coded by Gregory Senyshyn.

Privacy

Wolverine Fitness uses Google Analytics, including its Demographic and Interest reporting, to find out how the site is being used so that we can improve it.

We will never give away or sell our Analytics information. It will only be used by us to improve the site.

If you would prefer to opt out of Analytics, you can use the Google Analytics Browser Add-ons without issue. Blocking Javascript, however, will prevent this site from working properly
