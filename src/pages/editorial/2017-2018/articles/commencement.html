Wellington Heights 2016-17 Commencement

On Friday, November 3rd, Wellington Heights Secondary School held its 13th annual commencement.

The graduating class totaled 152 students (some of which were unfortunately unable to attend commencement), which is comparatively larger than the graduating classes of recent years. Over the course of the night, more than sixty awards were presented to Wellington Heights students, both graduating and still attending the school.
